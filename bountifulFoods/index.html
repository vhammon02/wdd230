Hi! Welcome to Bountiful Foods! We are a buisness that brings organic, locally sourced foods, to the tables of Carlsbad, California and the rest of the Costal Southern California region. We sell fresh fruit and produce directly from the source in our store and foodtrucks. Our mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options, including organic, made to order, fruit drinks and bags to the people of Costal Southern California.

Summary of Southern California

The Costal Southern California region is home to some of the most beutiful beaches in the world. From San Diego, to Orange County, and up to Los Angeles there are many amazing beaches and sights. This region is famous for it's large and diverse population and the gorgeous coastal climate. With many sandy beaches and perfect waves for surfing it is a world famous getaway.
