Audience

Persona 1

Picture

Demographics

"Maya" is a 37-year-old small business owner that is looking to make a store in our town.

Motivation

Maya is motivated by the need to expand her business and to be a strong part of the community.

She would like to network with other buisnesses and investors to get to know the area better and become important to the community

Maya needs a way to learn about and contact the local chamber of commerce to achieve her goals.

Demographics

Motivation

Gregory is motivated by a sense of community and contribution to the community

Gregory wants to be more involved in the community and to learn about current businesses involved in the chamber of commerce/p>
